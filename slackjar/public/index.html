How it works

Pay freelancers and contractors within Slack using the cryptocurrency of your choice. The recipient can choose to receive the funds in the cryptocurrency of their choice.

Commands

/splack [Slack username] [$ Amount] [Coin] Allows you to send BCH BTC ETH or LTC to a Slack user
/set_address [BCH address] Sets your BCH address
/check_balance [Account] Checks your coinbase balance
/splack_help Lists all of commands
